- Cavaliere, G., Nielsen, M. Ø., Gonçalves, S., Zanelli, E., <a href="https://www.tandfonline.com/doi/full/10.1080/01621459.2023.2284980">Bootstrap Inference in the Presence of Bias</a>, <b><i>Journal of the American Statistical Association</i></b>, forthcoming. <br>


<br>


We consider bootstrap inference for asymptotically biased estimators. We show that the proper implementations of the bootstrap can deliver valid inference bypassing bias estimation. The procedure is based on the prepivoting approach of Beran (1987, 1988), originally proposed to deliver higher-order refinements. We propose two different implementations of prepivoting and show the practical relevance of the results through five specific examples.
